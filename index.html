OUR TEAM

Marc Schneidman

Marc Schneidman, Founder, Chief Investment Officer and Managing Member

Marc is the Portfolio Manager for Aquilo Capital Management. He has over 20 years experience in biotechnology research and investing. Previously, Marc was on the three person portfolio management team at the Biotechnology Value Fund (1995-2009). Marc has served on the Board of Directors of several private biotechnology companies. Marc is a graduate of the University of California, Santa Barbara with a B.A. in Biological Sciences and has a J.D. from Santa Clara University.

CHIEF INVESTMENT OFFICER

Adam Bristol, PhD

Adam Bristol PhD, Director of Research

Adam is the Director of Research at Aquilo Capital Management. He joined Aquilo in January 2010. Prior to joining Aquilo, he served as an Associate at the Biotechology Value Fund, assisting in sourcing and evaluating private and public biotechnology companies. Adam is a Board member of the Excellence in Investing for Children's Causes foundation and has served as an advisor to the Michael J. Fox Foundation for Parkinson’s Research. He is a Class 14 Kauffman Fellow and was formerly on the Board of Directors of Airmid and Viriome Inc. Adam was a postdoctoral fellow in Neurobiology at Stanford University’s School of Medicine and has a PhD from Yale University.

DIRECTOR OF RESEARCH

Lee Seward

Lee Seward, Director of Operations and Marketing

Lee is the Director of Operations and Marketing at Aquilo Capital Management. He joined Aquilo in January 2014. Previously, he held the position of Managing Director at Credit Suisse, where he was responsible for Institutional Equity Sales and Sales Management roles for the Western region. Lee is on the Alive & Free/Omega Boys Club Advisory Board and a former Drew School Board of Directors member. He has an MBA from the University of Virginia and is a graduate from Duke University with an B.A. in Economics.

DIRECTOR OF OPERATIONS & MARKETING

Patrick Rivers

Patrick Rivers, Research Analyst

Patrick joined Aquilo Capital in June of 2014. From 2005-2014 he worked as a research scientist in various drug development capacities for several public and private biotech firms, including Sutro Biopharma, Exelixis and Xoma. This experience includes research and development for preclinical and clinical stage products in oncology and inflammatory diseases. Patrick received his B.S. in Chemical Engineering at UCLA and his MBA from UC Berkeley’s Haas School of Business.

RESEARCH ANALYST

Cariad Chester

Cariad Chester, Research Analyst

Cariad joined Aquilo Capital in March of 2016. Previously, Cariad served as a research scientist in the lab of Dr. Holbrook Kohrt at Stanford University. His research focused on understanding tumor-immune system interactions during cancer onset, progression, and treatment. He has authored or co-authored over 20 peer-reviewed scientific manuscripts and presented research at conferences in Canada, the US, and China. Before his research career, Cariad worked in clinical trial management, helping to launch and coordinate phase I, first-in-human trials of investigational immunotherapeutic agents. Cariad received his B.A. from Swarthmore College, where he double majored in Neuroscience and Comparative Religious Studies.
